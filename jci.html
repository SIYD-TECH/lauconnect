About JCIN LAUTECH

Junior Chamber International Nigeria (JCIN) LAUTECH Chapter is a vibrant community of young active citizens dedicated to leadership development and community service. Through impactful projects, workshops, and collaborations, members learn to become changemakers who create sustainable impact within and beyond the university.

As part of the global JCI network, JCIN LAUTECH offers opportunities for networking, leadership training, and participation in life-changing initiatives that foster growth and excellence.
